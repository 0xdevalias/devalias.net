Strength in solitude.
A slow walk down grey paths.
Shafts of fleeting colour,
Impressing on the mind.
Bathed in stillness.
Feeling complete,
Or lost in grey?
How can one separate,
Themselves from their bias
And see their truth whole
From the inside?
